I am fully compliant with the Data Protection Act 2018.

My Approach

I take a holistic and evidence-based approach to health, focusing on the root cause of any imbalance rather than just the symptoms. I offer general advice aimed at preventing illness and promoting good health and wellbeing, as well as more personalised advice tailored to individual need. I recognise that no one dietary or lifestyle programme fits all, so I will create a personalised plan which works with your personality and daily commitments, so that you'll find it easy to maintain long-term. I use psychological coaching strategies to empower clients to make positive lifestyle changes.

Tests

Where appropriate, I can organise relevant laboratory tests to be carried out to identify any biochemical imbalances in the body.

Supplements

Nutritional supplements are only recommended on the basis of clinical need and the latest evidence-based research.

Ongoing Professional Development

As part of my ongoing continuing practice development, I have attended numerous training courses over the years including those with specific focus on diabetes, weight control and digestive health.

Consultations

Consultations can be face-to-face, online or over the telephone. I provide email support post-consultation to ensure you stay on track with your health goals.
